Patrick McGuire

McGuire Digital Design & Development, Lead Sign Tech Image360 South Tampa — Jefferson County Born Native

I'm not a voter in Ohio's 11th, I'm states away now... but Ohio is my home state, and when a woman from Ohio says she's going to fight, you better believe it's going to be a slobberknocker. I've worked with Cortney for only a short time, but that's all it takes to see who she is: a woman who knows exactly what she stands for, says it plainly, and doesn't back down. She's brave in a way that most people only talk about. It takes real courage to stand up and stand out.. especially when you're fighting for the marginalized, the overlooked, and the working people who've been ignored for too long. She's not doing this for a career. She's doing this because somebody has to... and she decided it was going to be her.
