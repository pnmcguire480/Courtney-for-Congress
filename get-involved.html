Priority: Ballot Signatures

As an independent candidate, Cortney isn't in the primary. To get on the November general election ballot, we need to collect 2,200 valid signatures and submit them to the Board of Elections by May 4th, 2026.

If you live in Ohio's 11th Congressional District, your signature gets Cortney on the ballot. Volunteer to help canvass for signatures in your neighborhood.
