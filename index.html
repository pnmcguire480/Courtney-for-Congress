Who She Is

Meet Cortney

A nurse and mom fighting for working families in Ohio's 11th district. Cortney grew up downtown Ashtabula, Ohio in a hardworking family. She's spent the last 12 years working as a licensed practical nurse, with a diverse career including work as an Anesthesia Technician, Pediatric Outpatient Nurse, and Cardiac Data Abstractor for the National Cardiovascular Data Registry CathPCI Program.

A rare condition required a stent to be placed in her brain to restore normal pressure and blood flow. After her procedure in October 2025, she's feeling better than she has in years ‚Äî and that radical change fueled her passion to help people on a larger scale.

My Guiding Principle: The Golden Rule

Living by the golden rule, I treat others with the same respect I seek, uphold others' dignity, live my life with integrity, speak out against injustice, and protect those who cannot defend themselves.

A natural helper and fierce protector, Cortney once believed nursing was her true calling. But as national crises deepen, she's come to realize her lifelong defense of the "little guy" has been preparing her for public service all along.

"I am NOT a politician."

Instead, I want to be a POLICYMAKER and someone who fights for fairness and equity for the working class. I have had seasons of feast and seasons of famine. I have two wonderful sons, and I am a proud pet parent to 4 rescues. I am joining the fight to eliminate corruption in DC and to champion Quality of Life for all Americans.
