This Agreement is between Patrick Nicholas McGuire ("Developer") and Cortney Peterson, acting as both candidate and treasurer of the authorized campaign committee identified above ("Client"), for website design and development services in connection with the Client's congressional campaign.
1 Scope of Work

Developer will provide website-related services for the Client's campaign, including:

  • Website design and layout
  • Website development and setup
  • Integration of campaign-provided content, links, and media
  • Reasonable launch support for the initial deployment

This Agreement covers the current website build and launch only, unless the parties later agree in writing to additional work.

2 Fee and Payment
Project Fee: $900.00 total  ·  Payable in monthly installments of $325.00

The total fee for the website design, development, and launch described in this Agreement is $900.00. Client agrees to pay this amount in monthly installments of $325.00, beginning on the date agreed by the parties, until the total project fee of $900.00 has been met or surpassed.

Client may cancel this Agreement at any time before the full project fee has been paid. If Client cancels early, Client owes nothing beyond the installments already paid as of the cancellation date. Developer accepts the risk that the full project fee may not be reached.

Payment shall be made by the campaign committee. Developer will provide an invoice or receipt for each installment describing the services as website design and development, web services, or similar campaign-related vendor work.

3 Ownership and Rights

The campaign shall retain ownership and control of its domain name, campaign branding, campaign text, campaign images, and other campaign-specific materials supplied by or approved by the Client.

Upon payment of the full $900.00 project fee, Client shall have the right to continue using the finished website deliverables created for the campaign, including the deployed site files and campaign-specific design work prepared under this Agreement, subject to any third-party services or licenses used in the build. If Client cancels before the project fee is fully paid, Client retains the right to use the website as delivered up to that point.

Developer retains ownership of any pre-existing tools, code libraries, templates, workflows, or general-purpose design and development methods used to create the site, but grants Client a perpetual license to use them as incorporated into the campaign website delivered under this Agreement.

4 Hosting, Third-Party Services, and Accounts

If hosting, domain registration, form handling, analytics, fonts, or other third-party tools are used, those services may be subject to separate terms and costs charged by the relevant provider.

If reasonably possible, campaign-owned accounts, domain access, and essential credentials should be maintained in the campaign's name or under campaign control.

5 Post-Launch Updates and Maintenance

This Agreement does not include ongoing maintenance beyond the initial build and launch.

Once the full project fee of $900.00 has been paid, Client may elect to continue receiving website maintenance services from Developer. Maintenance includes routine edits, content updates, troubleshooting, and minor additions as reasonably needed to keep the campaign website current.

Maintenance Rate: $75.00 per month  ·  Begins only after the $900 project fee is fully paid

Client may cancel maintenance at any time with written notice and owes nothing further beyond the current billing period.

Upon cancellation or termination of maintenance, Developer will reasonably cooperate in transferring the site and related access back to the campaign or its designated representative.

6 Campaign Approval and Compliance

Client is responsible for reviewing and approving all final website content before launch, including factual content, issue statements, endorsements, donation language, and any legally required disclaimer language.

Developer is not serving as campaign counsel and is not providing legal advice. Client is solely responsible for confirming that all disclaimers, reporting, and campaign-finance requirements are satisfied before launch and during use of the website, and is encouraged to consult legal counsel if needed.
7 Recordkeeping

The parties intend for this arrangement to be documented as a normal campaign vendor transaction. Developer will provide reasonable documentation of the services performed and amounts charged. Client is responsible for its own reporting, bookkeeping, and compliance obligations.

8 Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time upon written notice.

If Client terminates before the full project fee has been paid, Client owes nothing beyond installments already paid as of the termination date. No early-termination penalty or remaining-balance obligation applies.

If the project fee has been paid in full, Developer will provide the Client with the then-current website files and reasonable handoff information necessary for continued campaign use, excluding Developer's separate internal tools or unrelated proprietary materials.

Termination of maintenance services follows the same process: Client may cancel at any time with written notice, owing nothing beyond the current billing period.

9 Entire Agreement

This Agreement is the entire agreement between the parties for this project and may be amended only in writing.
