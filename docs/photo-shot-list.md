# Cortney for Congress — Campaign Photography Shot List

> **Purpose:** Guide for a photographer or the campaign to capture the images needed to bring the website to life.
> **Current state:** The site has only 2 photos (headshot + wide headshot). It needs ~50-70 images to feel complete.
> **Last updated:** March 12, 2026

---

## The Narrative Arc

Voters should see Cortney as:
1. **A real person** — mom, nurse, neighbor
2. **A fighter** — rallies, speaking, passion
3. **A listener** — community events, one-on-ones, coffee conversations
4. **A leader** — podium, crowds behind her, statehouse testimony

The photos should tell that story across every page.

## Technical Requirements

- Shoot at highest resolution, compress to under 200KB for web
- Strip EXIF data before publishing (privacy + security)
- Provide portrait AND landscape crops of key shots
- Warm color temperature to match site's cream/plum/coral palette
- Alt text written for every image (accessibility + SEO)
- No stock-photo energy — documentary, not corporate
- No AI-generated images
- No American flag backgrounds — visual language is "Ohio working class" not "generic politician"
- No shots where she's alone in a crowd — always engaging with people
