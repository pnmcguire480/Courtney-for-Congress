## WHAT'S MISSING OR WEAK

### A. Homepage doesn't answer "#1 question: Why her and not the incumbent?"
Meet Cortney is 90% biography, 10% argument. Voters need to understand in 10 seconds: who, why running, what differently.

### B. No "Why Independent?" section anywhere
Single biggest gap. Every voter will ask "Why not run as a Democrat?" — no answer exists on the site.

### C. Opponent never named
"Incumbent (OH-11)" throughout Follow the Money. Weakens SEO and voter connection. Name her — it's factual, not negative.

### D. No email capture above the fold
Email signup buried in footer. Grassroots campaigns live and die by the email list.

### E. No share buttons anywhere
No social sharing on blog posts, Follow the Money, or issues. This is how zero-budget campaigns go viral.

### F. Only one blog post published
Three drafts sitting in `docs/drafts/`. Campaign in signature phase needs weekly updates minimum.
