# Content Calendar — Cortney Peterson for Congress

## How This Works

Cortney texts/sends notes about a topic. Patrick (or Claude) turns it into an article.

### To publish an article:
1. Copy `_template.html` to a new file named `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.html`
2. Fill in the placeholders (title, description, date, content)
3. Add a card to `news/index.html` (most recent at top)
4. Add the URL to `sitemap.xml`
5. Commit and push — Netlify deploys automatically

### SEO rules for every article:
- Title must include a target keyword (see calendar below)
- Mention "Ohio's 11th District" or a specific city (Cleveland, Lakewood, etc.) at least once
- Keep meta description under 160 characters
- Link to at least one other page on the site (issues, get-involved, events)
- Publish on site FIRST, then share to social with a link back
